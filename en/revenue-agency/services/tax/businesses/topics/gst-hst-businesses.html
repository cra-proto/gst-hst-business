Get your access code in your CRA account

As of October 20, 2025, business owners can view their GST/HST access code through their secure CRA account (My Business Account only).

Temporary SINs starting with 9 must register with BRO

If your temporary Social Insurance Number (SIN) starts with 9, use Business Registration Online (BRO) to register and instantly get a business number (BN) and CRA program accounts. For details: Important information for individuals with a temporary SIN starting with 9.
